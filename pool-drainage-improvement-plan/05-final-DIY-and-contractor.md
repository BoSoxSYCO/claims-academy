# FORMAT B — CONTRACTOR SCOPE OF WORK

Hand this to 2–3 drainage/landscape contractors for **apples-to-apples bids.** Each line is a required deliverable + spec.

**Project:** Backyard stormwater management to eliminate runoff/sediment intrusion into the pool. Corner lot, sandy soil. Discharge to the **street (left side); gravity confirmed.**

**Scope of work — contractor to furnish & install:**

1. **Fence-line interceptor (A ≈ *40 LF*):** French drain along the vinyl-fence base — 4" perforated pipe in ¾" washed stone, fully wrapped in non-woven geotextile ("burrito"), min 1% fall to the conveyance main; cleanout at high end.
2. **Perimeter deck-edge drain (B ≈ *60 LF*) — NEW:** remove existing decorative rock at the deck edge; install pre-fab channel drain w/ grate, set flush in concrete along the pool-deck uphill edge; tie to a **catch basin with a deep sediment sump** at the low point. *(No usable drain exists today.)*
3. **Regrade (C ≈ *1,500 SF*):** re-grade the lawn to positive fall **away from the pool deck** toward the interceptor/moat and the street; no ponding. Import screened fill as needed; compact.
4. **Downspout capture (D = *2*):** connect roof downspouts to solid pipe; route to the conveyance main.
5. **Conveyance to street (E ≈ *60 LF*):** 4" solid SDR-35 pipe, ¼"/ft target (1% min), in-line cleanout, **pop-up emitter** at the right-of-way; comply with municipal right-of-way discharge rules.
6. **Soil stabilization:** sod all disturbed/bare areas (C); erosion-control blanket on slopes (*≈400 SF*).
7. **Testing & closeout:** flood-test at the fence line and demonstrate flow to the street with **no water reaching the pool**; provide as-built sketch of pipe runs, basin, and cleanout locations.

**Bidder requirements / questions to compare:**
- [ ] Licensed & insured; pulls any required stormwater/right-of-way permit
- [ ] Confirms **811** locate before excavation
- [ ] Line-item pricing per scope item 1–7 (so you can trim scope if needed)
- [ ] Pipe/stone/fabric specs and slopes stated in the bid
- [ ] Warranty on workmanship (drainage function) — how long?
- [ ] Restoration included (sod, cleanup, haul-off)

**Suggested phasing if budget-staged:** Item 2 + 4 (deck edge + downspouts) → Item 6 (sod) → Item 1 (interceptor) → Items 3 + 5 (regrade + street line). Items 1, 4, 6 are also the DIY-friendly pieces if you want a hybrid split.
