# Pool Drainage Improvement Plan

A phased engineering project to stop stormwater, sand, and sediment from washing into a
backyard swimming pool on a Florida corner lot during heavy rain.

## Problem summary
During heavy rain, runoff from a hill behind the white vinyl fence sheets across a bare,
sandy back lawn, passes a low decorative knee wall, crosses the pool deck, and drains into
the pool — carrying sand and dirt with it. The pool is currently the low point of the yard;
the street (the intended outlet) is on the opposite (left) side.
