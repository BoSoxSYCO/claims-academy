# Case 0001 — Parked Vehicle Collision

## Claim Type
Auto Physical Damage

---

## Loss Summary

The insured reports that their vehicle was struck while parked outside a grocery store.

The vehicle was unoccupied at the time of loss.

The insured discovered the damage upon returning to the parking lot.

---

## Key Facts (Known at FNOL)

- Vehicle was parked legally in a grocery store parking lot
- Time of loss is estimated but not confirmed
- No witnesses identified yet
- No police report confirmed at FNOL
- Damage reported to rear bumper and left quarter panel
- Vehicle condition: drivable
- No injuries reported

---

## Unknowns

- Identity of at-fault party
- Whether surveillance footage exists
- Exact time of impact
- Whether a hit-and-run occurred
- Whether police report will be filed

---

## Initial Adjuster Actions

1. Confirm policy coverage
2. Obtain recorded statement
3. Inspect vehicle or assign inspection
4. Check for surveillance opportunities
5. Identify potential witnesses
6. Confirm parking lot ownership and security cameras

---

## Training Objective

Students will learn to:

- Conduct FNOL intake based on real claim facts
- Separate known vs unknown information
- Identify next investigative steps
- Avoid premature fault determination
