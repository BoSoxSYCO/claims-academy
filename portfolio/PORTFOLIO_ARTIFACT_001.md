# Portfolio Artifact 001 — FNOL Intake Documentation

---

## Purpose

This artifact demonstrates the student’s ability to properly document a First Notice of Loss (FNOL) using professional claims standards.

---

## Scenario

The insured reports:

> “My parked car was hit while I was inside a grocery store.”

Vehicle was located in a parking lot. Damage reported to rear bumper. No injuries.

---

## Student Submission Requirements

Write a complete FNOL intake note including:

- Policy information (placeholder acceptable)
- Date and time of loss (estimate if unknown)
- Location of loss
- Description of incident
- Parties involved
- Vehicle damage
- Witness information (if any)
- Police report status
- Initial next steps

---

## Evaluation Criteria

Submission will be graded on:

- Objectivity (no assumptions)
- Completeness
- Professional tone
- Proper separation of fact vs unknown
- Structured documentation format

---

## Example Format (Do Not Copy)

FNOL Intake Summary:

- Reported by: Insured
- Loss description: Vehicle struck while parked at grocery store
- Damage: Rear bumper reported damaged
- Injuries: None reported
- Police: Not confirmed
- Witnesses: None reported

---

## Learning Outcome

Students demonstrate readiness to:

- Handle real FNOL intake scenarios
- Document claims professionally
- Avoid premature conclusions
- Prepare files for investigation
